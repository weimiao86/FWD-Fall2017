Colorado Seasons

Home Scenery Activities

Spring

  • Enjoy Colorado's millions of acres of natural lands in its parks, monuments, and forests.
  • Hike Colorado trails through wildflower meadows, cliffs, canyons and much more.
  • Experience Colorado with a visit to a dude ranch

Summer

  • Whitewater rafting is one of the most exciting ways to enjoy Colorado's magnificent rivers.
  • There’s no better way to get close to Colorado’s abundant wilderness areas than by camping under the stars.
  • Take advantage of dozens of endurance and high-altitude races throughout Colorado.

Fall

  • Climb a Colorado 14er or enjoy a lazy creekside amble on Colorado's hiking trails.
  • Enjoy Colorado trails and scenery on a horseback ride.
  • In Colorado, bicycle riding is not just a way to get around: It’s a way of life. Enjoy Colorado's abundance of bike trails for all levels.

Winter

  • Hit the slopes skiing or snowboarding in any of Colorado’s 27 ski and snowboard resorts.
  • Combine the excitement of a high-speed ride with the beautiful scenery during a Colorado snowmobiling adventure.
  • Bundle up the kids (and adults) and head for the hills where you’ll find plenty of sledding and tubing in Colorado.
